# Pushpaak O 2 - Company Profile

## Company Identity
Pushpaak O 2 is an Indian aerospace company developing indigenous aviation platforms and unmanned aerial systems.

## Leadership
- **Mr. Aditya Shrivastava** – President & Co-Founder (Strategic Vision, Governance)
- **Mr. Aneerudh Kumar** – Co-Founder & Technology Lead (Engineering, Systems Architecture)

## Location
Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India

## Mission
100% indigenous aerospace innovation with DGCA compliance and sustainable aviation solutions.

## Core Focus
- Fixed-Wing & Hybrid Aircraft
- Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS)
- AI-enabled autonomous flight systems
- Hydrogen fuel cell technology
- Urban air mobility solutions
