# Pushpak O2 - Company Profile

## Company Identity
Pushpak O2 is an Indian aerospace company developing indigenous aviation platforms and unmanned aerial systems.

## Leadership
- **Mr. Aditya Shrivastava** – President & Co-Founder (Strategic Vision, Governance, Business Development)
- **Mr. Aneerudh Kumar** – Co-Founder & Technology Lead (Engineering, Systems Architecture, R&D)

## Co-Founder Details
### Mr. Aditya Shrivastava (President & Co-Founder)
- Role: Strategic leadership and business operations
- Responsibilities: Company governance, partnerships, regulatory compliance
- Focus: Market expansion and organizational growth

### Mr. Aneerudh Kumar (Co-Founder & Technology Lead)
- Role: Technical leadership and innovation
- Responsibilities: Engineering design, system architecture, product development
- Focus: Technology advancement and prototype development

## Location
Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India

## Mission
100% indigenous aerospace innovation with DGCA compliance and sustainable aviation solutions.

## Core Focus
- Fixed-Wing & Hybrid Aircraft
- Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS)
- AI-enabled autonomous flight systems
- Hydrogen fuel cell technology
- Urban air mobility solutions
