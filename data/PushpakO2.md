# Pushpak O2 - Complete Company & Project Profile

## 1. Company Identity
Pushpak O2 is an Indian aerospace and advanced engineering company dedicated to designing, developing, and deploying indigenous aviation platforms and intelligent aerial systems. It emphasizes complete in-house development with full control over design, IP, manufacturing, and system integration. The company's platforms include fixed-wing and hybrid aircraft, unmanned aerial systems (UAS), advanced drones, and urban air mobility solutions that meet DGCA & safety compliance standards.

## 3. Core Technologies & Products
Pushpak O2 develops advanced aviation systems and intelligent platforms including:
- **Fixed-Wing & Hybrid Aircraft**  
- **Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS)** for surveillance and monitoring  
- **AI-enabled autonomous flight systems** with real-time obstacle detection

These technologies leverage sustainable hydrogen fuel cells and AI autonomy to enable zero-emission, long-range operations.

## 4. "Pushpak" - The Vehicle (Technical Specifications)

### Vehicle Overview
"Pushpak" (by Pushpak O2) is an advanced futuristic drone-like aerial mobility vehicle designed for urban air transportation.

### Key Specifications
- **Load Capacity**: 500 kilograms total
- **Seating Configuration**: 4 persons total
  - 1 Pilot (fixed cockpit position)
  - 3 Passengers
- **Purpose**: Short-distance air transportation
- **Design Focus**: Compact, lightweight, and efficient

### Design Features
- **Aerodynamically Optimized**: For balance and energy efficiency
- **Safety-First Approach**: Stability and smooth air travel prioritized
- **Comfortable Interior**: Designed for visibility and ease of access
- **Pilot Control**: Fixed cockpit with navigation and control systems

### Innovation Areas
- Urban air mobility solutions
- Future transportation systems
- Sustainable aviation technology
- Advanced flight control systems
